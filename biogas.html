What is Biogas


Biogas is a renewable energy source produced by the anaerobic digestion of organic matter, such as food waste and livestock manure. It primarily consists of methane and carbon dioxide, and can be used for electricity generation, heating, and as a vehicle fuel. Additionally, the byproduct (digestate) serves as a nutrient-rich fertilizer, promoting sustainable agriculture.
